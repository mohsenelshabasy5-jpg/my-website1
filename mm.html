Al Ahly is one of the biggest football clubs in Egypt and Africa.It was founded in 1907 in cairo and known as the "Club of the century in Africa " because of its many continental titles. Al Ahly has a very large fan base inside and outside Egypt. The team is known for its red and black colors, and its main stadiums are Cairo International studium and Al Salam Stadium.
Zamalek is one of the most famous football clubs in Egypt and Africa. It was founded in 1911 in Cairo. The club is known for its white jersey with a red horizontal stripe. Zamalek has a strong and passionate fan base across Egypt. The team plays its home matches mainly at Cairo International Stadium.
Ismaily is one of the well-known football clubs in Egypt. It was founded in 1924 in the city of Ismailia, near the Suez Canal. The club is famous for its yellow kit and is often called “The Dervishes” because of its unique and skillful playing style. Ismaily has a loyal fan base, especially in the Suez Canal region. The team plays its home matches at Ismailia Stadium.
Al Masry is one of the historic football clubs in Egypt. It was founded in 1920 in the city of Port Said, located on the Suez Canal. The club is known for its green and white colors and for having a strong and passionate fan base that is deeply connected to the identity and culture of Port Said. Al Masry plays its home matches at Port Said Stadium
